Overview
Sayfty is a a non-profit organization, empowering women in India against gender violence. Sayfty has the vision to make the daily lives of millions of Indian women safer by empowering them to take a stance against gender violence. My Ambar which currently supports over 100,000 Indian women, is a free to use app for anyone who is looking for information, advice or support on surviving and healing from gender based violence The app aims to provide urgent help with SOS and Camouflage options, Demystify Legal Complexities by simplifying the language for it's users, laying down critical information in a form that is easy to comprehend.
